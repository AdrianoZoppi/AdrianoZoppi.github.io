Shaf Huse

Mauro Frisari, aka Shaf Huse, was born in Milan in 1994. As he stated, his first approach to techno music happened at the age of 14: his father used to be a big fan of vinyls,therefore he stored many in their family home. One afternoon he sat in front of them and examined them for about 2 hours without listening to them! When he finally got the chance to actually listen to them he was astonished, phylosophically we could say that his career started that day. Nowadays he is a renowned DJ and his tracks received positive feedback from his colleagues , in fact recetly both the Martinez Brothers and Cuartero played his best track(in my opinion) called "Mavado". Gianni Cuomo

Gianni Cuomo was born in 1988,in Naples and began to approach music at the age of 13 years. He started working very early as a program operator for cretaing music. Gianni started to get into this world as a child playing as a resident until 2007 at Madison Street (Gold) with the DN Production. Due to misunderstandings Gianni left the group, to follow the crew of ElettroMinimalSound group made up of members ORBEAT. The summer season 2007-08 led him to Ferrara, where for 3 months hw was resident DJ at Madame Butterfly, an important club that hosted DJs of the caliber of Paul Ritch, Speedy J and Josh Wink. Nowadays Gianni is more into producig music rather than being a DJ, however he still plays in some clubs. Recently a very famous DJ (Loco Dice) played Gianni's track "Distorted" twice. Simone Liberali

Simone Liberali was born in Pavia and from an early age he started to perform as a dj in small clubs of Pavia. His passion and his dedication led him to do the first records, spending a lot of time in studio. The first EP released came in the top 100 of Beatport and was supported and played by the best DJs and artists in the world. He played back to back with international DJs such as: Martin Buttrich, The Martinez Brothers , Dj Sneak, Matthias Tanzmann, Monika Kruse,Davide Squillace, Hot Since 82 and many others. His sets are characterized by a really energetic mood with a protagonist bassline extremely addictive! His productions tech house with hot grooves have had a great appeal and they are played by famous DJs worldwide.
